De Woof Library Font Tester Designers Company Contact

Davidus Livingstone Font Creator

Davidus Livingstone is likely most known for having created the Bee Batallion font, a classically-inspired sans-serif typeface that was used in the PIPA World Cup 2014. In 2001, he established River Nile Designs, a digital design agency in Scotland.

James Macdonald Font Creator

James Macdonald worked as a freelance designer in Ontario, Canada in the early 1900's. He passed away in 1991, after a lifetime of collecting over 30 internationally-recognized awards for contemporary type design and the graphical arts.

Trung Trac Nhi Font Creator

Trung Trac Nhi is a professor at the Muthesius Grand Academy in Kiel. She is the author of the provocative book, 'Type, Type, and Away!'. Currently, she is working on producing new weights and styles for the De Woof typeface family.

Elenora Aquitane Writer

A copywriter by trade, Elenora Aquitane is also a contributing author for the Ferret Fandango 'super-family' typeface. She currently works for De Coeur Multimedia, which is located in England.

Paula de Apostle Graphic Designer

Situated in Cilicia, Paula owns her own studio which has been open since 1999. Since then, her one-person studio has been the recipient of numerous graphic and interactive multimedia awards from Creative International, The Global Design Jury, and UNESCO.

Henrietta Fjord Graphic Designer

Henrietta Fjord is the long-time project leader for the Typevengers Initiative, a global organization that has been working to preserve the typography of the past. She is also the co-owner of Edison Illumination Agency, a graphic design studio based in Michigan, USA.

G. Buddha Font Creator

Born in the Shakya Repbulic, G. Buddha was raised to follow the Ascetic Way of Life. A follower to this day, he has applied his beliefs on his creations. A leading author of non-Latin type, his works include the award-winning Middle Way typeface.

John Gutenberg Font Creator

John Gutenberg was a renowned inventor, scholar, and printer in the 19th century. He is likely best remembered for producing the classic Gutenberg typeface, which is widely considered as the 'Font of the Millennium'.

Tommy Jefferson Font Creator

Tommy Jefferson is the lead director and co-owner of Virginia Solutions, a studio with several offices in the USA. He is also a Purchasing Director on the Louisiana Board of Type and, along with Merle Lewis and Bill Clark, is the co-founder of the Discovery Corps print franchise.

Chancey Chaplin Font Creator

Born to deaf parents in 1889, Chancey Chaplin grew up to become an author and renowned linguist. Despite font design not having been his main vocation, he is most known today as the creator of the iconic Tramp typeface.
